About Us

The family behind the flavor.

Family Owned. Minneapolis Made.

Deez Philly's is a family-owned business rooted in Minneapolis. We bring the bold, cheesy, loaded flavors of a classic Philly cheesesteak with our own Minnesota twist.

You can find us at U.S. Bank Stadium on game days, serving up our signature MN Style Philly to thousands of hungry fans. Every sandwich is made fresh with quality ingredients and a whole lot of love.

We're not just a food stall — we're a family showing up together, building something we're proud of, one cheesesteak at a time.

What Makes Us Different

Our MN Style Philly isn't your average cheesesteak. We load it up with premium steak, melted provolone, caramelized onions, and our signature toppings that keep fans coming back every game.

We believe great food comes from great people — and our crew brings the energy, the smiles, and the hustle every single time we fire up the grill.
